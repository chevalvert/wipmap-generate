wipmap-generate

Usage:
  wipmap-generate <X> <Y>
  wipmap-generate -x=<X> -y<Y>
  wipmap-generate <X> <Y> -o map.json
    
Options:
  -h, --help                    Show this screen.
  -v, --version                 Print the current version.
  -o, --output <file>           Write generated map in <file>.
